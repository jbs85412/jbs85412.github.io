FLORIDA

Florida has been my family's favorite vacation spot for along time. We have been going there since I was a baby and I actually celebrated my first birthday there. We have traveled there with many different people like our extended family, friends, even my soccer and volleyball teams for tournaments.
Our normal vacation spot is Santa Rosa Beach, Florida which is on the panhandle of Florida in the Gulf of Mexico. It is beteen Destin and Panama City Beach and has the clearest waters and the whitest sand. It is a beautiful and fun place that my family and I will go to for a long time.
CALIFORNIA

My sister and I went to California this spring break to visit one of our good friends. We flew into San Diego then drove to Palo Alto where he goes to school at Stanford University. We visited a lot of the places in San Diego and also went to San Francisco to see the Golden Gate Bridge. We were not there for long, but it was a fun trip.
UTAH

My family and four other families rented a house in Salt Lake City, Utah for a week for a winter break skiing trip. All my best friends were there and this was my first time learning how to ski. We would wake up at 9:00 to start our day of skiing. It was so much fun and the scenery was beautiful. I cannot wait to go back there again soon.
